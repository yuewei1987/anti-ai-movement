# Contributing to Anti-AI Movement

We welcome contributions from everyone who shares our vision of protecting human labor in the age of AI.

## How can you help?

### 1. Translations
We want this manifesto to reach every worker on the planet. If you are fluent in a language not yet supported, please:
- Create a new directory under `locales/` (e.g., `locales/ja/`, `locales/fr/`).
- Copy the contents of `locales/en/MANIFESTO.md` and translate it.
- Submit a Pull Request.

### 2. Share Stories
If your workplace or industry is being negatively impacted by AI, feel free to share your story (anonymously if needed) by opening an issue or a discussion.

### 3. Legal and Policy Research
Help us draft more specific policy demands or research existing labor laws that can be used to protect workers against AI-driven displacement.

### 4. Technical Support
Help us maintain this repository and build tools to track AI impact on employment.

## Guidelines
- Stay respectful and inclusive.
- Focus on labor rights and ethical technology.
- Avoid harassment or hate speech against individuals.

# 贡献指南

我们欢迎所有认同保护劳动者愿景的人加入。

## 你可以如何帮助？

### 1. 翻译
我们希望这份宣言能触达全球每一位劳动者。如果你精通尚未支持的语言：
- 在 `locales/` 下创建一个新目录。
- 翻译宣言并提交 PR。

### 2. 分享故事
如果你的行业正受到 AI 的负面影响，欢迎通过 Issue 或 Discussion 分享你的故事。

### 3. 法律与政策研究
帮助我们起草更具体的政策诉求，或研究现有的劳动法如何应用于 AI 背景下的劳动者保护。
