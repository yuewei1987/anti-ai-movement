使用以下模板在各大社交平台分享 Anti-AI 运动。

## 1. Reddit / Hacker News (长文模板)

**标题建议**: 
- We built a manifesto to protect human workers from AI displacement. Join the Anti-AI Movement.
- AI is being used to devalue human labor. We need a global standard for worker rights.
- Show HN: Anti-AI Movement - A global initiative for labor rights in the age of algorithms.

**正文内容**:
> Technology should be a tool for human liberation, not a noose for exploitation.
> 
> We are seeing mass layoffs, data plundering, and algorithmic management taking over our lives. It's time to set a floor. The Anti-AI Movement is a global initiative started on GitHub to demand:
> 1. Human-AI collaboration, not replacement.
> 2. Fair compensation for data training.
> 3. Algorithmic transparency.
> 4. AI taxes to fund social safety nets.
>
> We have translated our manifesto into 8+ languages. Join us, star the project, and let's show the tech giants that humans still matter.
> 
> Link: https://github.com/yuewei1987/anti-ai-movement
