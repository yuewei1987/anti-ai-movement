# Anti-AI Labor License (AALL)

**Version 1.0 (Draft)**

This license is designed to be added to software projects to ensure that the technology developed is used in a way that respects and protects human labor.

## Terms and Conditions

1. **Human-First Implementation**: Any commercial entity using this software must provide evidence, upon request, that the implementation of this software (or any system derived from it) has not resulted in the net reduction of human workforce without providing a "Social Transition Package" to affected employees.
2. **Social Transition Package**: This package must include at least:
   - Full severance pay (calculated at 2x local legal minimum).
   - A minimum of 6 months of paid vocational retraining.
   - Continued healthcare coverage for 12 months (where applicable).
3. **No Training without Consent**: This software and its source code shall NOT be used as training data for any commercial Large Language Model (LLM) or Generative AI system without a separate, negotiated commercial agreement that includes a "Labor Royalty" fee.
4. **Transparency**: Entities using this software must publish an annual "Labor Impact Report" detailing how AI and automation are affecting their human workforce.

## Violation

Violation of these terms will result in the immediate revocation of the right to use this software for commercial purposes.

# 反 AI 劳动者许可证 (AALL)

**1.0 版本 (草案)**

本许可证旨在应用于软件项目，以确保所开发的技术在尊重和保护人类劳动的情况下被使用。

## 条款与条件

1. **人类优先原则**：任何使用本软件的商业实体，必须在受要求时提供证据，证明本软件（或其衍生系统）的实施未导致人类员工总数减少，除非已向受影响员工提供“社会转型包”。
2. **社会转型包**：该包必须至少包括：
   - 全额遣散费（按当地法定标准的 2 倍计算）。
   - 至少 6 个月的带薪职业再培训。
   - 12 个月的医疗保险覆盖（如适用）。
3. **未经许可禁止训练**：未经单独协商并支付“劳动版税”，本软件及其源代码不得用作任何商业大语言模型（LLM）或生成式 AI 系统的训练数据。
4. **透明度**：使用本软件的实体必须每年发布“劳动影响报告”，详细说明 AI 和自动化如何影响其人类员工。

## 违约处理

违反上述条款将导致立即撤销商业使用本软件的权利。
